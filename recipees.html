Healthy Recipes

Introduction

Here are some of my favorite healthy recipes that I enjoy making to stay fit and energized. These recipes are easy to make, nutritious, and delicious!

Recipes

  • Avocado Toast: Mash one ripe avocado, spread on whole grain toast, and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  • Grilled Chicken Salad: Grill a chicken breast and mix it with a salad of spinach, tomatoes, cucumbers, and olive oil dressing.
  • Quinoa Bowl: Cook quinoa, then add roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and a tahini-lemon dressing for a tasty, balanced meal.
  • Protein Smoothie: Blend a banana, spinach, protein powder, and almond milk for a refreshing and nutritious drink.
  • Overnight Oats: Mix oats with almond milk and chia seeds, leave in the fridge overnight, and top with fruits and nuts in the morning.
