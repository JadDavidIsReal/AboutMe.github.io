Healthy Recipes

Avocado Toast with Egg

A simple yet satisfying breakfast. Toast a slice of whole-grain bread, mash half an avocado on top, and finish with a fried egg and a sprinkle of chili flakes.

Spicy Roasted Chickpea Salad

A flavorful and filling lunch. Toss chickpeas with olive oil and spices, roast until crispy, and serve over a bed of mixed greens with your favorite vegetables.

Oven-Baked Salmon

A healthy and delicious dinner. Season a salmon fillet with lemon, dill, and garlic, then bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes. Serve with roasted asparagus and quinoa.

Apple Slices with Almond Butter

A quick and easy snack. Simply slice an apple and serve with a side of almond butter for a dose of healthy fats and fiber.
